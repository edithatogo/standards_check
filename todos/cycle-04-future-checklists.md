# Cycle 04: Future Checklists

This document tracks reporting checklists that are currently in development and are planned for future inclusion in the `standards_check` project.

- PRISMA-AI (Artificial Intelligence)
- PRISMA-C / PRISMA-PC (Children)
- PRISMA-EconEval (Economic Evaluations)
- PRISMA-PF (Prognostic Factors)
- PRISMA-Prev (Prevalence)
- PRISMA-QES (Qualitative Evidence Syntheses)
- PRISMA-RR (Rapid Reviews)
- CONSORT for Crossover Trials
- CONSORT for Multi-arm Trials
- CONSORT for Surrogate Endpoints
- CONSORT for Chinese Herbal Medicine Formulas
